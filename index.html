Folded Futures

This summer, we visited a public school and worked with underprivileged children to build their realistic and ideal dream rooms — using cardboard, glaze paper, clay and markers. This site documents their drawings, interviews, and the stories behind their creations.

Our Mission

Folded Futures documents creative expression and resilience. We provided materials (cardboard, glaze paper, clay, markers) and simple prompts so children could design model rooms — both realistic and ideal. Our goal is to share their art and stories to raise awareness and celebrate imagination.

Project details
  • Location: Local public school
  • Materials: cardboard, clay, glaze paper, markers
  • Participants: Students (age groups vary)
